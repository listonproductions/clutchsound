Now tracking Record · Mix · Master · Shoot · Stream

A working studio for sound and picture, in equal measure.

Clutch was founded in 2005 by producer Brian Liston. In 2020 the whole operation moved into an enormous purpose-built space on Coyier Lane — load-in door, parking lot, and room to actually work.

We record, mix and master for albums, advertisements, film and broadcast. We shoot in-studio and in the field. And we stream it live when you need it live.

Since 2005 · The Smart Studios lineage
We took over the room where Nirvana, Garbage and the Smashing Pumpkins were made — then we outgrew it.

Brian Liston moved Clutch into the former Smart Studios after Butch Vig sold the studio and headed to California. After several fruitful years in that historic building, it was simply too small for modern production.

We did some great recordings there, and had some good parties too. We're making better recordings now — and the parties are still happening.

— Brian Liston, Founder & Producer
