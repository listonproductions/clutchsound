Stage F

The Stage

40′ × 40′ for video, stills & live audio

A 40-by-40 open stage for full bands, film and photography.

Stage F is the big room — a 40′ × 40′ open floor that swallows a full drum kit, a live band, a film set or a photo shoot without breaking a sweat. Its analogue front end fans out to dozens of numbered inputs, so a whole ensemble can be tracked live in one pass.

High ceilings, flexible lighting and a clear load path from the garage make it equally at home for music, motion and stills.
