Isolation E

Iso / Live Booth

Tracking, voice-over & dub

A warm, isolated room for vocals, voice-over and intimate tracking.

Isolation E is a tight, quiet booth dressed with stone, soft furniture and house instruments — an upright piano, guitars and keys within arm’s reach. It’s ideal for lead vocals, voice-over, dub and dialogue, or any source that needs to be kept clean and separate.

A window connects it straight back to Control C, so performer and engineer stay in eye contact through every take.
